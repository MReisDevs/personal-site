The Curious Case of the C Language

It is often said that a web developer's learning is never done and that by the time you know everything there is to know about a language, it's too late because it's obsolete. And over the course of the last 50 years these statements have held mostly true. One curious exception however is the C language which has survived even as other languages have been built to replace it and then faded into irrelevance themselves. Check out this article to learn more about the curiously prolific life of C: The Curious Case of the Longevity of C
