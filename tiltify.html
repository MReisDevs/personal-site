About you

You're changing an industry

Before Tiltify, fundraising on Twitch was a difficult process to get right. If you collected the money through a payment processor, people didn't get the credit they deserved from an organization. If you directed people to the organization or campaign's page, they didn't get the credit or recognition they deserved on stream. Then came Tiltify and your company provided an application that allows people to create a crowdfunding fundraising campaign with the ability to track funds raised, offer incentives, and most importantly provide on screen alerts based on donations received. Start to finish, your application does better in one place what streamers previously had to manage in several applications.


You're looking for innovators

As Tiltify continues to grow, it needs people who are mavericks in their specialty. As the only company that does livestream fundraising, there's no roadmap to follow and every employee is forging a path through unexplored territory. More importantly, for Tiltify to maintain that growth, you need driven individuals who work hard and love to do it. The perfect match for the job wants to learn new skills and technologies for their own satisfaction and integrate that new knowledge into the code they write.
